project updates

MB2: Theory of Mind

Data collection is underway! Sign-up HERE 
Contact Tobi Schuwerk with questions.

Make sure to attend our talk during the MB: Empirical Projects symposium at the Big Team Science Conference.

MB3: Rule Learning

Data collection has started! Sign-up HERE
We're also looking for mentors to help new labs prepare for testing. If you are willing to help (or have questions), please contact Ingmar Visser.

Don't miss our talk during the MB: Empirical Projects symposium at BTSCON.

Project Lead Claartje Levelt will be at BUCLD. Contact her if you're interested in meeting up!

MB3N: Rule Learning fNIRS

The team successfully presented an update at the fNIRS Conference and will also be presenting at the upcoming Big Team Science Conference. Don't miss it!

MB4: Social Evaluation

We are looking for labs to join the project to help collect data! Call for Data Collection, sign-up here, and read more about the project in the MB4 Big Manual. If you have questions, contact Kelsey Lucca or Francis Yuen.

Yiyi Wang will be presenting at the upcoming Big Team Science Conference. Stop by her talk to learn more about MB4 and spin-off projects!

MB5: Hunter & Ames Model of Infant Looking Preference

Progress continues on stimulus development and manuscript drafting. Join our MB5 Slack workspace for updates and to join the discussion. 

We'll be presenting as part of the MB: Empirical Projects symposium at BTSCON; we hope to see you there!

MB-AtHome: Online Infant Data Collection

The translations team is looking for help translating study materials. Please contact Sho Tsuji if you are interested.

Don't miss our talk during the MB: Methodological Projects symposium at BTSCON!

MB-Demographics 

Project Leads and working group members will be meeting soon to discuss updates and progress. 

We'll be presenting at BTSCON as part of the MB: Methodological Projects symposium and hope to see many of you there!

MB-ManyWebcams 

The team has finished data collection and is preparing for final analyses and manuscript writing. Contact Tobi Schuwerk with questions.

We're also participating in the MB: Methodological Projects symposium at BTSCON and can't wait to tell you about our progress.
