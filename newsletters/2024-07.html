BTSCON submissions due August 9
The submission deadline for the 2024 Big Team Science Conference has been extended to Friday, August 9. If you have any big team science work you'd like to present (in progress work is welcome), make sure to get those submissions in!

ManyBabiesies Award winners
We were thrilled to announce winners of the 2nd not-so-annual ManyBabies Awards (now known as The ManyBabiesies) at the recent ICIS conference in Glasgow. Congrats to all of the winners and to everyone who attended the awards "ceremony" in Glasgow. It was so wonderful to celebrate our amazing MB community!
