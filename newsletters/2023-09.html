Data collection is in full swing for MB3! Currently, 21 labs have been greenlit, for a total sample size of over 450 infants. We continue to welcome labs to sign up and contribute data for the main project as well as the MB3T spin-off project. Please check the MB3T Lab Manual and email Anna Exner to indicate your interest. 

MB researchers have submitted together for an MB workshop at BCCCD in Budapest, Jan 4-6 covering MB3, MB3NIRS & MB3T. MB3T will be presented at the BTS conference October 23-25, online.
